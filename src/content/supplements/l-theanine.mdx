## What it is

L-theanine is an amino acid found naturally in tea, associated with a state of
relaxed alertness. It is best known for being paired with caffeine to smooth out
the jitters.

## What the evidence shows

**Calm focus, alone.** <EvidenceTier tier="preliminary" basis="human" />
The evidence points to a subtle calming effect on attention rather than anything
dramatic. It is not a sedative and doesn't typically cause drowsiness.

**Paired with caffeine.** <EvidenceTier tier="preliminary" basis="human" />
Some studies support smoother, less jittery attention from the caffeine-plus-
theanine combination than caffeine alone, though effects are modest.

## Who it suits

People who like caffeine's focus but dislike its edge, and anyone after a gentle
attention aid with a good tolerability profile.

## Practical notes

It is one of the better-tolerated supplements and is commonly taken alongside
coffee or a pre-workout. If caffeine timing is your real issue, our
[caffeine calculator](/caffeine-calculator) shows how long it lingers in your
system.
