## What it is

Tribulus terrestris is a plant extract and one of the most common ingredients in
"testosterone booster" products. Its popularity rests on a long-standing
reputation and a compelling marketing story rather than on results.

## What the evidence shows

**Testosterone, strength and muscle.** <EvidenceTier tier="marketing-claim" basis="human" />
Controlled studies in men generally show it does not meaningfully raise
testosterone or improve strength or muscle. The reputation has simply outlasted
the disappointing evidence.

## Who it suits

There is little reason to take it for its headline claim, which the evidence
does not support.

## Practical notes

As a herbal extract, quality and content vary between brands, and there are
isolated reports of liver and kidney problems with some tribulus products. Given
the lack of benefit, there is little reason to take on that risk. See our note on
[testosterone boosters](/supplements/testosterone-booster) for the wider
category.
