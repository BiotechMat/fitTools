## Methodology

The Pace of Aging Index is an **open composite index** (v1.0.0), a
transparent 0 to 100 score summarising how favourable your current lifestyle
trajectory looks. It is explicitly **not a biological age**; it's built
from modifiable habits and simple measures, framed as levers you can move.

Each input becomes a 0 to 100 sub-score (higher = more favourable) via a
documented clamped-linear mapping, then combined by weight:

Cardiorespiratory fitness carries the most weight in the design, but our
dedicated fitness-age estimate is on hold pending a source we can verify,
so **VO₂max is optional here** (enter a known value, or skip it). Grip and
HRV are optional too. Leave any optional input blank and the weights
renormalise across what you provided, with a reduced-confidence flag. Full
house methodology is on the [index methodology page](/learn/index-methodology).

The **pace** number is a centred display of the same index (~0.8 favourable
to ~1.2 less favourable), a display transform, not a hidden model.

## Interpreting your result

Look at the **radar and sub-scores**, not just the headline. They show which
habits are lifting or dragging your trajectory, and every one of them is
modifiable. A low sub-score is a next step, never a verdict. Body-composition
(waist-to-height) is deliberately capped at 12% so the score can never be
dominated by, or reward chasing, a single body measurement.

Track your own trend. Because the weights and mappings are fixed and
versioned, a change in your score reflects a real change in your habits.

## Limitations

- This is a **self-tracking construct, not a biological age and not a
  medical test**; it hasn't been validated against health outcomes.
- The sub-score anchors are reasonable but chosen by us and published so you
  can judge them; different anchors would give different numbers.
- Cardio fitness, the input with the strongest evidence, is optional
  pending a verifiable source, which lowers confidence when it's omitted.
- It describes lifestyle, not destiny: genetics and circumstance matter
  enormously and aren't captured here.
