# 200 Common Supplements

A reference list of 200 widely used supplements, grouped by category, with a one-line note on what each is typically taken for. This is a neutral reference, not an endorsement or dosing guide — evidence strength varies enormously across this list, and several entries are popular without strong support. Always check interactions and regulatory status before use.

## Protein & amino acids (1–30)

1. **Whey protein (concentrate)** — Fast-digesting dairy protein for muscle protein synthesis.
2. **Whey protein isolate** — Higher-purity whey, lower in lactose and fat.
3. **Whey protein hydrolysate** — Pre-digested whey for faster absorption.
4. **Casein protein** — Slow-digesting dairy protein, often taken before sleep.
5. **Micellar casein** — Minimally processed slow-release casein.
6. **Milk protein (blend)** — Combined whey/casein blend.
7. **Soy protein isolate** — Complete plant protein.
8. **Pea protein** — Popular vegan protein, high in leucine.
9. **Rice protein** — Vegan protein, often blended with pea.
10. **Hemp protein** — Plant protein with fibre and omega fats.
11. **Egg white protein** — Dairy-free complete animal protein.
12. **Collagen peptides** — Connective-tissue protein for skin, joints, hair, nails.
13. **BCAAs** — Leucine, isoleucine, valine; marketed for muscle preservation.
14. **EAAs** — Full spectrum of essential amino acids.
15. **L-Leucine** — Key trigger amino acid for muscle protein synthesis.
16. **L-Isoleucine** — Branched-chain amino acid.
17. **L-Valine** — Branched-chain amino acid.
18. **L-Glutamine** — Conditionally essential amino acid, gut and recovery claims.
19. **L-Citrulline** — Nitric-oxide precursor for pumps and blood flow.
20. **Citrulline malate** — Citrulline bonded to malate, common pre-workout.
21. **L-Arginine** — Nitric-oxide precursor (poorer absorption than citrulline).
22. **Beta-alanine** — Buffers muscle acidity; causes tingling.
23. **Taurine** — Amino acid for endurance, hydration and heart claims.
24. **L-Carnitine** — Fatty-acid transport, marketed for fat metabolism.
25. **Acetyl-L-carnitine (ALCAR)** — Brain-penetrant carnitine form.
26. **L-Carnitine L-tartrate** — Recovery-oriented carnitine form.
27. **Glycine** — Amino acid for sleep, collagen and glutathione synthesis.
28. **L-Tyrosine** — Dopamine/noradrenaline precursor for stress and focus.
29. **L-Tryptophan** — Serotonin precursor for mood and sleep.
30. **HMB (β-hydroxy β-methylbutyrate)** — Leucine metabolite, muscle-preservation claims.
